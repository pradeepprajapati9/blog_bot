What is Digilocker & How to Use It for Digital Documents in India

27 Jun 2026

In today's digital age, carrying physical copies of important documents can be a hassle, and there's always the risk of loss or damage. The Indian government's Digilocker initiative is a revolutionary step towards solving this very problem. As a key part of the 'Digital India' program, Digilocker provides a secure, cloud-based platform for the issuance, storage, and verification of your essential documents and certificates. From your Aadhaar card to your driving license, vehicle registration, and even academic certificates, Digilocker makes them accessible anytime, anywhere, with full legal validity. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about Digilocker and how to harness its power for convenient and secure document management.

What is Digilocker and Why You Need It

Digilocker is a secure cloud-based platform for storing, sharing, and verifying your documents and certificates digitally. Launched by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, it aims to eliminate the need for physical documents. Imagine never having to worry about misplacing your driving license or scrambling for your PAN card details when applying for a service. Digilocker provides dedicated cloud space linked to your Aadhaar number, where government agencies can directly push electronic copies of your documents (like your Class 10th mark sheet, vehicle registration, or even vaccine certificates). These digitally issued documents are legally equivalent to their physical counterparts under the IT Act, 2000. It offers unparalleled convenience, enhances security by reducing fraud, and promotes paperless governance.

Getting Started: Registering for Your Digilocker Account

Setting up your Digilocker account is a straightforward process. First, download the official Digilocker app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, or visit the Digilocker website (digilocker.gov.in). On the homepage or app, look for the 'Sign Up' option. You will be prompted to enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number. This is crucial as Digilocker links your identity to your documents. After entering your Aadhaar, an OTP (One Time Password) will be sent to the mobile number linked with your Aadhaar. Enter this OTP to verify your identity. Next, you'll be asked to set a 6-digit security PIN for future logins, adding an extra layer of protection. Once these steps are completed, your Digilocker account is successfully created, and you are ready to explore its features.

Issuing Documents: Accessing Government-Issued Certificates

The 'Issued Documents' section is where you can fetch official electronic copies of documents directly from the issuing authorities. This includes your Aadhaar card, Driving License, Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC), PAN card, academic certificates (from CBSE, universities, etc.), and even insurance policies. To issue a document, navigate to the 'Issued Documents' section in the app or website. You can then search for the specific issuing authority (e.g., Ministry of Road Transport & Highways for DL/RC) or the document type. Once selected, you'll typically need to enter a reference number (like your DL number, PAN number, or roll number) along with your name and date of birth. Digilocker will then fetch the document directly from the source and save an authenticated e-copy in your account. These documents carry the same legal weight as their physical counterparts and can be presented to authorities when required.

Uploading Your Own Documents: The 'Uploaded Documents' Section

Beyond government-issued documents, Digilocker also allows you to upload and store your own documents securely in the 'Uploaded Documents' section. This is perfect for certificates or papers that aren't yet digitally integrated with Digilocker, such as property documents, old academic records, or personal receipts. To upload, go to the 'Uploaded Documents' section and select the 'Upload File' option. You can then browse and select files from your device. Digilocker supports various formats like PDF, JPEG, and PNG. It's advisable to categorize your uploaded documents (e.g., 'Education', 'Health', 'Property') and assign descriptive names for easy retrieval. While these user-uploaded documents do not carry the same 'issued' legal validation as those directly fetched from government sources, they provide a secure and accessible digital backup of your important papers.

Sharing and Accessing Documents On-The-Go

One of Digilocker's most compelling features is the ability to access and share your documents anytime, anywhere. Imagine being stopped by traffic police and simply showing your digital Driving License or RC from your phone. Similarly, when applying for a new job, a loan, or a rental agreement, you can securely share your relevant documents (e.g., PAN card, academic certificates) directly from your Digilocker account via email or other messaging platforms. This eliminates the need for photocopies and reduces the time spent on administrative tasks. The documents are digitally signed, ensuring authenticity. Just make sure your device has an internet connection to access your locker, and you're good to go. This convenience is a game-changer for everyday life and official interactions.

Security and Best Practices for Using Digilocker

Digilocker is built with robust security measures to protect your data. It uses 256-bit SSL encryption, and all data is stored in a secure cloud environment. Your Aadhaar-linked verification and the 6-digit security PIN ensure that only you can access your account. However, practicing good digital hygiene is crucial. Always keep your mobile number updated with Aadhaar and Digilocker to receive OTPs. Never share your login credentials (Aadhaar, PIN, OTP) with anyone. If you're using a public computer or device, ensure you log out of your Digilocker account immediately after use. Regularly check the 'Activity Logs' section in your account to monitor any access attempts. Keeping your app updated ensures you have the latest security patches. By following these simple best practices, you can maximize the security and benefits of your Digilocker account.

Conclusion

Digilocker is more than just a storage facility; it's a vital tool in India's digital transformation, offering unparalleled convenience, security, and authenticity for your personal and official documents. By embracing Digilocker, you're not just digitizing your papers; you're simplifying your life, reducing the risk of document loss, and contributing to a greener, paperless future. If you haven't already, take a few minutes to set up your Digilocker account today and experience the ease of having your entire document portfolio at your fingertips, safely and securely.
