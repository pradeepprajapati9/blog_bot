How to Get Personal Loan from Bajaj Finance

27 Jun 2026

A personal loan can be a helpful financial tool for various needs, from medical emergencies to home renovations. Bajaj Finance is a popular choice for many Indians seeking quick and convenient loan options. If you're wondering how to get a personal loan from Bajaj Finance, you've come to the right place. This guide will walk you through the entire process, from understanding eligibility criteria to submitting your application. We'll focus on providing clear, actionable steps to make your loan application journey smoother.

Bajaj Finance Personal Loan Eligibility

Before you apply, it’s crucial to know if you meet Bajaj Finance's personal loan eligibility criteria. Typically, you need to be an Indian resident, between 21 and 60 years of age. Your CIBIL score is a significant factor; a good score (generally above 650-700) increases your chances of approval. You should also have a stable source of income, whether salaried or self-employed. Salaried individuals usually need to be employed with their current organization for a minimum period, often 3 years in total and 1 year in the current job. Self-employed individuals need to demonstrate a consistent business vintage. Income thresholds vary, but a decent monthly income is essential to show your repayment capacity. Checking these basic requirements upfront can save you time and effort.

Documents Required for Bajaj Finance Personal Loan

Gathering the necessary documents is a key step in the application process. Bajaj Finance generally requires a set of standard documents to verify your identity, address, and income. These typically include proof of identity (like an Aadhaar card, PAN card, or passport), proof of address (like a utility bill or Aadhaar card), and proof of income. For salaried individuals, this means recent salary slips (usually the last 3 months) and bank statements (last 6 months) showing salary credits. Self-employed applicants will need to provide their Income Tax Returns (ITR) for the last 2-3 years, along with audited financial statements and bank statements. Ensure all documents are clear, valid, and up-to-date to avoid any delays in processing your loan application.

How to Apply Online for a Bajaj Finance Personal Loan

The most convenient way to apply for a personal loan from Bajaj Finance is online. Visit the official Bajaj Finance website and navigate to the personal loan section. You will usually find an 'Apply Now' button. Clicking this will take you to the application form. You’ll need to fill in your personal details, employment information, and loan requirements (loan amount and tenure). Bajaj Finance often offers pre-approved loan offers to existing customers, which can significantly speed up the process. If you're a new customer, you'll need to complete the full application form. Be prepared to upload scanned copies of your required documents through the online portal. Ensure you provide accurate information as any discrepancy can lead to rejection or delays.

Offline Application Process for Bajaj Finance

While the online route is preferred for speed and convenience, Bajaj Finance also offers an offline application process. You can visit a Bajaj Finance branch or a partner outlet to collect the personal loan application form. Fill out the form carefully with all the required details. You will then need to submit the completed form along with the photocopies of your supporting documents. A Bajaj Finance representative will guide you through this process. While this method might take a little longer than the online application, it's a good option if you prefer face-to-face interaction or are not comfortable with online processes. Ensure you get a receipt for your submitted application and documents.

Loan Amount, Interest Rates, and Repayment

Bajaj Finance offers personal loans for various amounts, typically ranging from ₹30,000 up to ₹25 lakh, depending on your eligibility and credit profile. The interest rates are competitive and vary based on factors like your CIBIL score, income, loan amount, and tenure. It’s important to understand the interest rate (often expressed as an Annual Percentage Rate or APR) and any associated processing fees or other charges. Once your loan is approved, you will be given a repayment schedule. Loans are usually repaid through Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs) over a tenure that can range from 12 to 60 months. You can choose a tenure that best suits your financial capacity, ensuring comfortable repayment without straining your budget.

Tips for a Successful Bajaj Finance Personal Loan Application

To increase your chances of getting approved for a personal loan from Bajaj Finance, a few tips can be very helpful. Firstly, ensure your CIBIL score is in good shape. A higher score demonstrates responsible credit behavior. Secondly, always provide accurate and complete information on your application. Any inconsistencies can lead to delays or rejection. Thirdly, be realistic about the loan amount you request; apply for an amount that you can comfortably repay. Fourthly, keep all your necessary documents ready and well-organized before you start the application process. Finally, if you have any doubts or queries, don't hesitate to contact Bajaj Finance customer care for assistance. Being well-prepared can significantly streamline your loan application.

Conclusion

Obtaining a personal loan from Bajaj Finance involves understanding their eligibility criteria, preparing the right documents, and choosing the application method that suits you best, whether online or offline. By following these steps and keeping our tips in mind, you can navigate the application process more effectively and increase your chances of a successful loan approval. Remember to review the loan terms and conditions carefully before finalizing your application to ensure it aligns with your financial goals and repayment capacity.
